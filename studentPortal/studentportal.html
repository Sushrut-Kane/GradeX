Calculate stress: Stress = Force / Area Force = mass * acceleration due to gravity = 5 kg * 9.8 m/s² = 49 N Area = 3 * 10^-6 m² So, stress = 49 N / (3 * 10^-6 m²) = 16.33 * 10^6 N/m² Y = stress / strain Y = (16.33 * 10^6 N/m²) / (2 * 10^-4) Y = 8.165 * 10^10 N/m²
